Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Offizone Coworking Space.

Offizone offers a variety of flexible workspace options including Coworking Desks, Dedicated Desks, Private Offices, Shared Offices, and a Professional Conference Room.
Our pricing varies based on the workspace type and duration (hourly, daily, monthly). For example, Coworking Desks are 2,000F/H, 6,000F/D, 60,000F/M. Private Offices are 10,000F/H, 25,000F/D, 200,000F/M. Shared Offices are 3,000F/H, 8,000F/D, 80,000F/M. The Conference Room (10-15 places) is 15,000F/H, 50,000F/D. All plans include essential amenities.
To book, please fill out our online booking form: https://offizone.cm/booking. Our team will review your request promptly and arrange payment before confirming your booking.
Offizone is located at Entrée Saint Marc Biyem assi, Directly opposite Platinum Lounge, Yaoundé, Cameroon. You can get directions here: Google Maps Link.
We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Offizone is closed on Sundays.
All workspace rentals include ultra-fast 100+ Mbps internet, big-screen computers on every desk, noise-cancelling headphones, printing facilities, complimentary tea/coffee, access to our relaxing lounge, and secure parking with CCTV.
